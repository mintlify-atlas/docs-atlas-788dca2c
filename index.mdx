Create is a Minecraft mod for NeoForge that redefines automation — not through menus and GUIs, but through animated, physical machines you build in the world. Gears turn, belts carry items, pistons push entire structures, and trains haul cargo across your world. Every system is visible, tangible, and yours to design.

## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install NeoForge">
    Create targets NeoForge for Minecraft 1.21.1. Install the matching NeoForge loader for your Minecraft version before adding Create.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download Create">
    Grab the latest release from [CurseForge](https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/create) or [Modrinth](https://modrinth.com/mod/create) and drop the `.jar` into your `mods/` folder.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn in-game with Ponder">
    Open any Create item's tooltip and press **W** (default) to open the interactive Ponder scene — animated, in-world tutorials for every block and mechanic.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build your first machine">
    Follow the [First Steps](/getting-started/first-steps) guide to craft a Hand Crank, connect shafts, and power your first Mechanical Press.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Looking for the Fabric port? The community-maintained Fabric version lives at [Fabricators-of-Create/Create](https://github.com/Fabricators-of-Create/Create).
</Note>
